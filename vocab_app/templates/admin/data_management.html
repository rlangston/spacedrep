Deletes master vocabulary words that are not active for any user. Related accepted answers, progress records, and answer history for those words are also deleted.

Deletes the entire master vocabulary list for everyone, plus all accepted answers, active testing sets, progress, quiz answers, and session history.

Will delete {{ stats.all_words }} words, {{ stats.all_sessions }} sessions, and {{ stats.all_answers }} answers.
